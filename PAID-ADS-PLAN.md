## Bidding & Match Type Strategy

- **Bidding:** Start with **Maximize Clicks** (first 2–3 weeks to gather data), then switch to **Target CPA** once you have 15–20 conversions
- **Match types:** Phrase match as default. Add exact match for your top 3–5 converters. No broad match until you have strong negative keyword lists built.
- **Device:** Bid up 20–30% on mobile (most people search from phones when arranging a shipment)
- **Schedule:** Bid up Mon–Fri 8am–6pm (business hours). Reduce bids 20% on weekends unless you see strong weekend conversion data.

---

## Conversion Tracking Setup (Do This First)

Before spending a dollar, set up:
1. **Google Ads → GA4 import** — import the `generate_lead` event as a conversion
2. **Call tracking** — import the `tel:` click event OR use CallRail with a Google Ads forwarding number
3. **Thank-you page** — fire conversion on `/thank-you` after form submission
4. **Target CPA goal** — aim for $40–$80 cost-per-lead (specialty shipping closes at 40–60%, so $80 lead cost = ~$160–$200 customer acquisition cost on a $300–$600 job)

## Integration with Organic SEO

- Every page you're buying traffic for already has **LocalBusiness schema, FAQ schema, and keyword-optimized copy** — Quality Scores will be high
- Use paid ads data to **confirm which pages convert best organically** — if pool table shipping converts at 12% on paid, prioritize it for link building too
- Pages with organic rank #1–3: **reduce paid bids** and reallocate budget to pages not yet ranking
- **Retargeting (optional Month 2+):** Run a Display retargeting campaign showing ads to people who visited the site but didn't convert. $50–$100/mo, very high ROI for specialty services.
