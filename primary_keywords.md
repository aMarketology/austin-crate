## 10. Keyword Strategy Notes

### Primary Target: Specialty Shipping Austin Texas
- The #1 keyword we want to dominate for all non-branded searches
- Used in: homepage H3, meta title, meta description, page.tsx keywords, badge text
- Supported by: all service pages, all location pages

### Secondary Focus: Custom Crating Austin TX / Furniture Crating Austin TX
- High-intent keywords with lower competition
- Used in: homepage badge, HomeClient tagline, furniture page H1, content-registry tags
- Supported by: buy-a-crate page, individual furniture sub-pages

### Long-tail Focus: Furniture Shipping and Crating Austin Texas
- Exact phrase targeted by furniture parent page and furniture sub-pages
- High commercial intent from interior designers, movers, luxury home buyers

### Local SEO Keywords (Round Rock / Cedar Park / Georgetown)
- Format: "specialty shipping [city] TX"
- Supporting: "specialty crating [city]", "furniture shipping [city]", "fine art shipping [city]"
- Each location page targets its own city cluster
